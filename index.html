I am an undergraduate student studying Biomedical Mechanical Engineering and Computing Technology in the University of Ottawa.

Projects

Whiteboard Writer

A team project for GNG1103 (Engineering Design). The AutoScribe uses strings and encoders to display whiteboard notes on Ross Video DashBoard. It also lets teams email their whiteboard to themselves.

Hip Exoskeleton

A team project for uOttawa Bionics, a multidisciplinary engineering team developing mechatronic and bionic devices to enhance quality of life and facilitate rehabilitation. The team is currently developing a 4-DOF hip-mounted exoskeleton to assist in the rehabilitation of stroke patients with reduced muscular strength.

Infant Resuscitation Manikin

A team project for the Biobuddy Design Competition.

Multicolour Pen
