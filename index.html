About Me

I'm an outcoming undergraduate student studying Biomedical Mechanical Engineering and Computing Technology in the University of Ottawa. I've always wanted to make an impact on society by improving quality of life through prosthetics and bionics.

I was the technical captain of uOttawa Bionics, a multidisciplinary engineering team developing mechatronic and bionic devices to enhance quality of life and facilitate rehabilitation.
